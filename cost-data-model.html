Business Cost Data Model & Reporting

Designed a business cost data model that transformed ERP transaction data into business-ready cost information, eliminating manual EY costing preparation and enabling direct planned-versus-actual cost analysis.

Business areaCost accounting
My roleData model & report design
SourceERP transactions and master data
OutputExcel cost analysis

01 · Business Challenge

Detailed ERP transactions were not organized for business cost analysis.

The existing process relied on monthly data exports and manual Excel preparation. One EY product could represent many ERP parts, while transaction-level records needed interpretation before users could compare cost and selling price.

Repetitive monthly preparation

Users rebuilt the EY cost analysis from exported ERP data.

Technical transaction detail

Raw transaction types were difficult to analyze directly.

Multiple parts per EY product

ERP product detail did not match the business reporting structure.

Manual cost comparison

Planned, actual, and selling-price comparisons required extensive manual work.
